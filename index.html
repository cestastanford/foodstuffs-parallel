Kitchen Records of the Königsberg Palace

A multidimensional explorer of Prussian foodstuffs. The parallel coordinates displays the amount of food in the database between 1641 and 1688, colored by food category. Brush the visualization to update other charts on this page.

Controls

Brush: Drag vertically along an axis.
Remove Brush: Tap the axis background.
